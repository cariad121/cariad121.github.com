Acorn Holistic Solutions

Providing freelance and private support to schools and families in helping children to engage in education and reach their true potential.

At Acorn Holistic Solutions, we passionately believe that everyone should have the opportunity to reach their potential.

It can be very difficult to support children, young people and their families to actively engage in education and to help children develop a positive commitment to learning. In these difficult times of austerity and efficiency savings, Schools and families are finding that many of the services that were designed to help them are either being decomissioned, criteria and thresholds made more demanding or some services now coming at a significant cost. In practical terms, Schools are now having to deal with many non-academic issues with very little help and support. Through necessity, Schools are being encouraged to become more pro-active and innovative in how they work with the behavioural, emotional and social difficulties presented by their pupils, and their families.

Addressing emerging issues before they become entrenched problems

The objective of the Green Acorn Children services is have a one stop shop resource for all non academic requirements of students and their families.
